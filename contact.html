Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Andhra Pradesh?

The best time to visit Andhra Pradesh is during the winter months from October to February when the weather is pleasant. For hill stations like Araku Valley and Lambasingi, November to January is ideal. Summer (March to June) is extremely hot, and monsoon (July to September) brings heavy rainfall to certain regions.

What documents do I need for Tirumala Temple visit?

For visiting Tirumala Temple, you need a valid government ID proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driver's License, or Passport). For darshan tickets and accommodation bookings, it's advisable to book in advance through the official TTD website. Our team can assist with these arrangements.

How do I reach Araku Valley?

Araku Valley is best accessed from Visakhapatnam. You can take the scenic train journey from Visakhapatnam to Araku (about 4-5 hours), or drive by road (approximately 116 km). The train ride is especially popular for its beautiful route through tunnels and valleys. We offer transportation packages from major cities to Araku Valley.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes, we specialize in creating customized tour packages based on your preferences, budget, and duration of stay. Whether you're interested in a spiritual journey to temples, adventure in Gandikota, or a relaxing beach vacation in Visakhapatnam, we can tailor a package specifically for you.

What language is spoken in Andhra Pradesh?

Telugu is the official language of Andhra Pradesh. However, in major tourist destinations and cities, English and Hindi are also widely understood. Our guides are multilingual and can communicate in Telugu, English, and Hindi to ensure a smooth experience for all tourists.
